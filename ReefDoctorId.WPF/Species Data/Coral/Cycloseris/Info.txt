All of the Fungidae can move to some extent, but Cycloseris are particularly known for being able to right themselves if overturned and being able to move over obstacles. This ability also makes them able to extract themselves from sediment, making them very successful in areas of high sedimentation where others may not survive.
Solitary.
Usually don't grow larger than 8cm.
Septa have fine teeth and the underside is almost smooth.
