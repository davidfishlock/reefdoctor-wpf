Leaf blade up to 15 cm long, 6-13 mm wide, with serrated margins
3-10 leaves per shoot, clustered at the top of a long stem (up to 60 cm) 
Leaf sheath 15-30 mm long, leaving visible scars on the stem
'Woody' rhizomes cream-coloured with internodes 1.5-3cm long, and with roots and stems arising at every fourth internode.
