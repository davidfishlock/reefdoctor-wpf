Rounded leaf tip with smooth margin (or minutely serrated in younger plants)
2-7 leaves per shoot, blades up to 15 cm, 2-4 mm wide with 9-15 veins
Well developed leaf sheaths up to 5 cm long forming a characteristic shaggy mass around base of shoot
Rhizomes pale pink; internodes 1-4 cm long, bearing a single shoot at each node
