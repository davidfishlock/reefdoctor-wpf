Shoots with up to 6 leaves, 10-40 cm long and up to 1cm wide
Distinctive by the presence of old sheaths forming a noticeable shaggy mass around the base of shoots
Rhizomes pale yellow, 3-5mm thick, internodes 4-7mm long partly covered by brown scales
Roots and shoots arising in an irregular manner from the rhizome, usually separated by four or more internodes
Slightly curved, speckled leaves
