Massive and dome shaped, attaining huge sizes (up to 2m high and 5m across) due to its very dense skeleton which is rarely attacked by boring organisms or grazed by fish. May form large flat colonies.
Corallites are plocoid.
Corallites are slightly raised and have a very distinctive shape. 
Septa are even and become thinner from the wall to the centre.
Polyps are extended only at night.
Only one species, Diploastrea heliopora.
