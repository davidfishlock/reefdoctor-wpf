Elongated snake like bodies
Moray eels - no pectoral/ventral fins, dorsal + anal fins join
Aggressive, head showing out of crevices by day
Snake eels:  thinner, hunt by day, pointed snouts
Conger eels: similar fins to morays but they have pectoral and dorsal fins
Garden eels: hide in burrows in the sand poke heads out of the holes, very shy
