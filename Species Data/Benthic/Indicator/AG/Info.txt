Algae can be green, red or brown in colour but for indicator survey you do not need to differentiate.
Turf algae is a fast growing algae frequently covering rock. For the survey you only record AG when your tape comes across turf algae if the algae is more than a few centimeters long.
Halimeda is a green algae that excretes a calcium carbonate 'skeleton', which makes it difficult to digest by many herbivores and so serves as protection from many algae-eating organisms.
